Women's Body

Women bear the brunt when revenge is to be sought, accounts have to be settled or punishment is to be awarded. Whether its adultery and rape, they both are seen as an insult to a man's honor.

About the project

Women bear the brunt when revenge is to be sought, accounts have to be settled and punishment is to be awarded. We keep our women in burkhas, ask them not to cross lakshman rekha or ask them to offered their necks to the sword for the sake of honor or question the legitimacy of a rape if she gets pregnant. There are stories where a woman's body is treated as property. But there are also stories where a woman's body is not treated as property, where women are seen as sovereign of their own lives. Through this wesite lets connect dots of woman's making through history in all the cultures. Let's make a time line "In The Name of Honor"..so that history in the name of shame never gets repeated again.
